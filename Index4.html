Computer Shortcuts To Computer Shortcut

1.Ctrl+Z: Undo. 2.Ctrl+W: Close. 3.Alt+Tab: Switch apps. 4.Alt+F4: Close apps. 5.Win+left arrow or Win+right arrow: Snap windows. 6.Win+Tab: Open the Task view. 7. Tab and Shift+Tab: Move backward and forward through options. 8.Ctrl+Esc: Open the Start menu. 9.Ctrl+C: copy 10. Crtl+V: paste
